Services provided by the GYM's are: -


STRENGTH WORKOUT


Strength training, often known as strength workout, is the practice of performing physical exercises that are intended to increase strength and endurance. It is frequently related with weight lifting.

YOGA CLASSES


Yoga is a practice that involves both the mind and the body. Yoga approaches vary in how they mix physical postures, breathing techniques, meditation, and relaxation. Movement, meditation, and breathing practices are used to promote mental and physical well-being.

CROSS FIT


Cross Fit is a strength and conditioning workout that consists of functional movement performed at a high intensity level. It is a type of high intensity interval training. These are acts that you do in your daily life, such as crouching, tugging, pushing, and so on.

CARDIO


Cardio is any sort of exercise that raises your heart rate and maintains it for an extended amount of time. As you begin to breathe faster and more deeply, your respiratory system will begin to work harder. Jumping rope, running stairs, walking, rowing, bicycling, and endurance training are all great cardio workouts for losing weight.

BOXING CLASSES


Most boxing courses are divided into three rounds, each of which includes an aggressive aerobic warm up, boxing with intervals of bodyweight exercises, and, of course, core work. All three rounds are extremely difficult and will leave you drenched in perspiration from head to toe. Boxing for fitness is also a wonderful cardio exercise. It can help to build bones and muscles, burn more calories, and improve one’s mood.

SESSION


Whether you want to burn fat or gain muscles. We offer an array of different fitness sessions to meet the dynamic needs of our clients. If you tried one type of class and didn’t love it, there’s probably still another fitness class that might be a game-changer for you.
Sessions we provide Zumba, Aerobics, Yoga, Tabata, and HIIT workout

HIGH INTENSITY INTERVAL (HIT)


High-intensity interval training (HIIT) is a training method that involves alternating short bursts of high or explosive anaerobic exercise with brief rest intervals till exhaustion. It refers to workouts that alternate between hard-charging intervals in which a person’s heart rate hits at least 80% of its maximum capacity for one to five minutes, followed by times of rest or less strenuous exercise.

SPECIAL YOGA CLASSES FOR PREGNANT WOMAN


Yoga is an exceptionally safe and beneficial activity for pregnant women since it can help prepare your body for labor and delivery in addition to offering relief throughout pregnancy. For pregnant women, prenatal yoga, hatha yoga, and restorative yoga are the best options.

TABATA


Tabata is a high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout that raises your heart rate and burns a lot of calories in a short period of time. Tabata increases athletic performance and glucose metabolism while also acting as a great fat-burning stimulus.
Sessions we provide Zumba, Aerobics, Yoga, Tabata, and HIIT workout
